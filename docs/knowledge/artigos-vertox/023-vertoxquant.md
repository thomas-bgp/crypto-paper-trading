Team Expansion and Collaborations

We have been expanding our team and are releasing more, longer and higher quality articles that cover a broader variety of topics! Especially in the area of infrastructure and code we’ve made a lot of improvements. Special thanks goes out to anyone that has contributed!

OnChain Statistical Arbitrage (BowTiedDevil)

We are collaborating with BowTiedDevil (@BowTiedDevil on X) who has written extensively about all things DeFi and OnChain trading.
